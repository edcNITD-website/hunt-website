    More about the Hunt

    EDC has stuck QR codes with edc logo all over campus, find as many as you can and earn points. When the esummit starts, the top 5 participants can exchange the points for prizes or benefits. The rarer the sticker you find is the more points! More information on the rules is given below.

    Rules

    • You can scan a QR only once, on scanning you get points according to the following criterias:
    • 1. If the QR you scanned has been scanned for the first time you get 50 points.
    • 2. If the QR you scanned has been scanned for less than equal to 5 times you get 15 points.
    • 3. If the QR you scanned has been scanned for more than 5 times you get 10 points.
    • 4. By sharing your invite code with friends on registering you and your friends get 10 points each for each new registration.
